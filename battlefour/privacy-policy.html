BattleFour Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

BattleFour does not include advertising, third-party analytics, or tracking. The developer does not operate a server to collect gameplay data from the app.

Data Stored on Your Device

BattleFour stores game settings, active game state, gameplay stats, selected content, unlock state, and purchase-related entitlement state on your device.

The developer does not collect, sell, or share this data.

Purchases

Optional purchases are handled by Apple through StoreKit. Apple processes the transaction, and the developer does not receive your payment card details.

The app stores purchase-related entitlement state on your device so purchased features can be unlocked.

Game Center

BattleFour supports Game Center leaderboards and achievements. If you are signed in to Game Center and use those features, the app may submit leaderboard scores and achievement progress to Apple Game Center.

Apple handles Game Center account information under your Apple account and Apple's policies.

Permissions

BattleFour does not request location, camera, microphone, photos, contacts, health, Bluetooth, or tracking permissions.

Contact

For support, use the support contact listed on BattleFour's App Store page.
