RunBob Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

RunBob is a simple run timer for families, coaches, and small groups. The app does not include advertising, third-party analytics, or tracking.

Data You Add

RunBob lets you save runner names, route names, route photos, runner photos, run times, and progress history. This data is stored on your device and may sync through your personal iCloud account when iCloud is available.

The developer does not collect, sell, or share this data.

Photos

Photos you choose for runners or routes stay in the app's local data and may sync through your personal iCloud account. RunBob does not upload those photos to a developer-operated server.

Contact

For support, use the support contact listed on RunBob's App Store page.
